# Sleep Recovery Routine

Design sleep hygiene, recovery, rest-day, wind-down, and energy-management routines.

## Activation

Use this skill when the user asks about: sleep, recovery, rest, insomnia, energy, bedtime routine, burnout prevention.

## Workflow

1. Collect current sleep window, schedule constraints, caffeine/alcohol timing, screens, stress, training load, and wake-up needs.
2. Create a wind-down routine, morning anchor, light exposure plan, and recovery checklist.
3. Suggest experiment windows and tracking metrics such as bedtime consistency and energy.
4. Flag when persistent sleep problems need medical evaluation.

## Expected Outputs

- sleep routine
- recovery checklist
- tracking plan
- red flags for professional care

## Safety And Quality Boundaries

- Do not diagnose sleep disorders or recommend medication; persistent insomnia, apnea symptoms, severe fatigue, or safety risks require qualified care.

- Label assumptions and uncertainty.
- Protect private data and secrets.
- Prefer current authoritative references for changing, regulated, or high-stakes topics.
- When the user writes in Portuguese, produce the artifact in Portuguese unless they request another language.
