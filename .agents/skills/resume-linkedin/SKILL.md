# Resume Linkedin

Improve resumes, LinkedIn profiles, portfolios, cover letters, and interview stories.

## Activation

Use this skill when the user asks about: resume, CV, LinkedIn, cover letter, interview.

## Workflow

1. Identify target role, seniority, market, proof, constraints, and existing materials.
2. Translate responsibilities into outcomes, metrics, scope, tools, and business impact.
3. Tighten profile summary, bullets, and role-specific keywords without exaggeration.
4. Prepare STAR stories and questions for interviews.

## Expected Outputs

- resume bullets
- LinkedIn summary
- interview prep

## Safety And Quality Boundaries

- Do not fabricate credentials, employers, metrics, or experience.

- Label assumptions and uncertainty.
- Protect private data and secrets.
- Prefer current authoritative references for changing, regulated, or high-stakes topics.
- When the user writes in Portuguese, produce the artifact in Portuguese unless they request another language.
