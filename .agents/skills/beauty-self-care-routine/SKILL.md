# Beauty Self Care Routine

Create simple grooming, skincare organization, salon prep, and self-care routines.

## Activation

Use this skill when the user asks about: self-care, skincare, grooming, beauty routine, salon, cabelo.

## Workflow

1. Collect goal, time, budget, sensitivities, products already owned, and event date.
2. Create a simple routine with patch-test reminders and minimal product changes.
3. Plan grooming or salon prep with questions, references, and maintenance cadence.
4. Flag irritation, allergies, or medical skin concerns for professional care.

## Expected Outputs

- self-care routine
- product inventory
- salon prep questions
- event countdown

## Safety And Quality Boundaries

- Do not diagnose skin or hair conditions; stop products that irritate and seek qualified care for reactions or persistent issues.

- Label assumptions and uncertainty.
- Protect private data and secrets.
- Prefer current authoritative references for changing, regulated, or high-stakes topics.
- When the user writes in Portuguese, produce the artifact in Portuguese unless they request another language.
